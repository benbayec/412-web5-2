Hall Rental

The St. Thomas à Becket Parish's large reception hall is available to rent for members and non-members of the parish.

The Hall can accommodate up to 275 guests and includes a full kitchen, tables and chairs, a sound amplification system and screen, and abundant free parking. The Hall is rented on an “as found” basis and the Lessee is responsible for returning the Hall in the same condition as that in which it was found.

The standard rental fee is $800. A separate refundable damage and cleaning deposit of $250. is also required.
