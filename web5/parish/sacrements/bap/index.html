Infant Baptism

Requirements:

  • Godparents must be baptized and confirmed Catholic or Orthodox (at least 1 must be Catholic), be at least 16 years of age, as well as be living a life in harmony with the faith.
  • Protestants are not admitted as official godparents, but they may act as official witnesses at the baptismal celebration.
  • One or two godparents are allowed, and if there are two, they must be of the opposite sex.

Required documents:

  • Copy of baby's birth certificate
  • Copy of godparent(s) confirmation certificate

When you call to book a date, be ready with the following information:

  • Name, birthdate, and birthplace of baby.
  • Names, address, and phone number(s) of parents
  • Name(s) of godparent(s) and current parish of worship
