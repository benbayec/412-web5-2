Adult Baptism, Communion and Confirmation

The Rite of the Christian Initiation of Adults (RCIA) is a guided conversion process by which non-baptized adults (catechumens) and baptized Christians (candidates) aged 19 and over are fully initiated into the Catholic Church.

RCIA is an opportunity for people to learn about the doctrine of the Catholic faith and enter into full communion with the Catholic Church.

The RCIA process is conducted by a team of lay church members and clergy over a seven-month period which begins every year in September and continues through Easter.
