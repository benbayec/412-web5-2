A Woman Named Alice

This is one of my earliest projects in this program. It was quite enjoyable, utilizing Photoshop to colorize a black and white picture was pretty sick.

Before

After

Enjoyment:

I had a lot fun learning the process of colorizng black and white photos, using the selective color ajustment to alter the grayscale in the picture was cool to learn and do.

Struggles:

Whilst it's fun to see the result of my work, I definetly had a few rough times with producing it. Masking was difficult for me to wrap my head around at first. Not to mention that I was using a device that rendered everything I would do at an extremely slow pace was quite infuriating.

Aftermath:

From this project, I then found a hobby of coloring not only vintage photos but also black and white manga that I read as well. They most likely helped in the development of my graphic design style.
