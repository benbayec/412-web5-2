The 10 Deadly Swordsmen

This fantasy album cover was made using Photoshop, we then printed the document and tried to piece it together to an actual, physical cover.

Front Cover

Back Cover

Enjoyment:

It was a very cool experience to materialize something from my imagination. The fact that we were given total creative freedom to create whatever we wanted was crazy to me. I like both Kendrick Lamar and the Wu-Tang Clan which made the work even more enjoyable.

Struggles:

It was definitely difficult to get this project done due to the device that I was using at the time, where my graphics would render at a very slow pace. Finding the visuals, brainstorming, typography were the many that I had trouble with during the process.
