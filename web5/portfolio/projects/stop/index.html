Home Works Bio

online classes be like...

This video was done for a stopmotion animation for my video editing class. During the pandemic I had a really hard time keeping up with school, I suppose this short story is a testament to it.

Enjoyment:

Coming up with the story in my head and watching it unfold into something tangible felt really great to me. Of course, Photoshop was used, but I also utilzed a sandbox game called GMOD, to create reference materials for certain objects in the video such as the phone and the bed.

Struggles:

The drawing process could've gone way faster if my device was better, especially when it came to the splatter effect, which would take at least 10-15 seconds to render. The editing process was a bit difficult as well since I was still new to Premiere. Unfortunately I couldn't salvage any analog sketches since they have been lost to time.
